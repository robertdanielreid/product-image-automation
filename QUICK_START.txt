1. Get a Gemini API key: https://aistudio.google.com/app/apikey
2. Double-click ONE_CLICK_SETUP.command and paste the key.
3. Double-click RUN_ALL.command.

If interrupted: double-click RESUME.command.
To see progress: double-click CHECK_STATUS.command.
To review uncertain images: double-click OPEN_REVIEW_QUEUE.command.
